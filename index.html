About our company

ComLinks Logistics is a professionally run transports logistics provider in East and Central Africa – with an enviable foothold of the freight handling market in Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Congo, and Southern Sudan from the UAE, Europe and America.

Our Vision

The ComLinks Logistics vision is to establish ourselves as the best logistics service provider by garnering the respect of our shippers and developing successful and satisfied customers and employees by focusing on our 3-Strengths RELIABILITY, SECURITY, TIMELINESS, acronymed as REST; the approach to solving our customers logistics processes and freight management needs, especially those who ship freight via less-than-truckload (LTL).

Our Mission

Our mission is to decrease freight management inefficiencies and to provide maximum hard and soft cost savings for our customers in the process of moving less than truckload-cargo or truckload freight and small package freight by providing logistics answers through our tested and proven transportation management procedures.
